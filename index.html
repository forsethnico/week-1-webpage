My Favorite Colorado Brewery

Avery Brewing

Avery Brewing was founded in 1993 in Boulder, CO by Adam Avery. In 1994, the Out of Bounds Stout took home GABF gold. Avery Tap Room opened in 2003. Avery has since introduced Barrel-Aged Series, an Annual Barrel Series, and has begun canning many of their beers. In 2015, Avery moved into their dream brewery shown above. In fact, in 2015 Avery was named Colorado Brewery of the Year by the Denver Post.
